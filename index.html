Fusion 360


When I first seen Fusion 360 I realized how cool it really was but it was very confusing at first. What I enjoyed the most was when you learn how to use it, it becomes very fun to use and play around with. Since it was my first time using Fusion 360 it was hard to understand what some of the keywords really meant and what they did. The design thinking behind my products was really something that applied to me and attracted me. But with my key chain I chose to put my brand name because it opens opportunities for design and potential accessories, then I had an idea to make it into a belt buckle because the 3D design made me be able to visualize my plan in real time. I will plan on using Fusion 360 for future interests and of course my brand.

The Handle.

When making my keychain my expectations for what you can do on Fusion 360 were definately higher than what was really possible. I had first used paint 3D to brainstorm what type of keychain I wanted. When I actually used Fusion 360 I realized that my brainstorm ideas were not longer what I wanted to make, so I went simplistic and made something from scratch. I knew that when it was printed I wanted my favorite color on the key chain, which is purple, so when it got printed I went simplistic and went with Black and Purple.
